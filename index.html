About Robotics Club

Club History

The Robotics Club at NIT Patna was established in 2012 with the vision of fostering innovation in robotics and automation among students. Over the years, we have grown to become one of the most active technical clubs on campus.

Mission

To provide students with hands-on experience in robotics, foster innovation, and prepare them for real-world challenges in automation and AI.

Vision

To be a leading student-led robotics innovation hub that contributes to technological advancement and societal development.

Objectives

  • Promote interdisciplinary learning in robotics
  • Participate in national/international competitions
  • Organize workshops and training sessions
  • Foster industry-academia collaboration

Club Activities

Competitions

We regularly participate in prestigious competitions like Robocon, Indian Robotics League, and various hackathons.

Workshops

From basics of Arduino to advanced ROS and computer vision, we conduct workshops for all skill levels.

Research Projects

We undertake research projects in collaboration with faculty members on cutting-edge robotics topics.

Infrastructure & Lab Facilities

Our club has access to a dedicated robotics lab equipped with:

  • 3D Printers and CNC machine for prototyping
  • Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and NVIDIA Jetson development kits
  • Various sensors, actuators, and robotic arms
  • High-performance computing workstations for simulation
  • Electronics workbench with oscilloscopes and function generators

Club Timeline & Milestones

2012: Club established with 15 founding members

2015: First participation in National Robocon

2018: Won first national competition (IIT Bombay Techfest)

2020: Established dedicated robotics lab on campus

2023: Published first research paper in international conference

2025: Secured 1st prize in National Robotics Championship
